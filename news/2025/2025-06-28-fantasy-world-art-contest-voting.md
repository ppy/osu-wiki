While our osu! mascots are having extravagant adventures, it's *your* duty to decide which portrayals of them are the most splendid!

![](/wiki/shared/news/2025-06-06-fantasy-world-art-contest/banner.jpg)

Having a total of **132** entries this time, our osu! resident artists have been busy creating a variety of fantastical worlds for our pippi and the gang to explore! Now, *you're* going to be tasked with an adventure of your own to crown the victors of this magical showdown with **15** votes!

Feeling overwhelmed with the sheer amount of submissions? Don't worry, we've got you covered with a slick slideshow showcasing every entry under the tunes of [Redside - Mainquest](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2351091#osu/5059397) grabbed right out of the [5 Digit World Cup 2025](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/2028931?n=1)!

As always, the following lineup of prizes will be up for grabs:

- A selection of the top-voted finalists will be **featured on the main menu**.
- Top-voted entries will receive **3 months of osu!supporter**.
- The top 3 will receive bespoke **profile badges** made from their own creations.
- The top 3 will also be featured as [**limited-time default profile banners**](https://osu.ppy.sh/home/news/2024-07-15-aerial-antics-art-results#new-profile-customisations), with the ones from last round rotating out.

Shoutout to all participants who've dedicated their time and effort into creating and submitting an entry — without you, we wouldn't have had a contest to begin with!

Good luck!
