Kasane Teto is the new queen of the vocaloid empire, and **d0tc0mmie** is here to spread the news to the masses.

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/493/header.jpg)

Vocaloid lovers here on osu! have been waiting for today. Hailing from Osaka, Japan, **d0tc0mmie** is a Teto connoisseur who dabbles in experimental interpretations of jungle, breakcore, EDM, and alternative rock. While **d0tc0mmie** has been an osu! player for 8 years, they're only classified as an osu! *Featured Artist* as of today.

A grand total of **12** songs have settled into their new home on [**d0tc0mmie**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/493)! Grab your pre-timed `.osz` while supplies last. (Note: supplies last forever.)
