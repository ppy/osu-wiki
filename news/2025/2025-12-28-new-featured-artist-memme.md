If you were tasked with listing the most legendary composers in the rhythm game scene, you'd find yourself writing down "**Memme**" sooner than later.

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/518/header.jpg)

Crafting classics since the 2000s, **Memme** is a South Korean producer whose works have been featured widely across the music game spectrum. While most titles only feature one or two of **Memme**'s tracks, *O2Jam* stands out as the exception: the majority of **Memme**'s rhythm game music (especially those recognisable by osu! players) were composed specifically for the *O2Jam* series.

And so we're bringing those iconic tracks to osu!.

Scour through all **19** available tracks on [**Memme**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/518), each pre-timed as the foundation for your next beatmap.

We'd confidently bet that you've played some of these maps before, but that doesn't mean you can't play them again. Relive memories or make new ones with everything our last Featured Artist of the year has to offer:
