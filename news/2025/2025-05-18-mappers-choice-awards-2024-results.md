After much anticipation, the community has spoken! Join us as we unveil the winners of this year's Mapper's Choice Awards.

![](/wiki/shared/news/2025-03-26-mappers-choice-awards-2024/banner.jpg)

In case you've missed the latest buzz in the mapping community, the Mappers' Choice Awards (MCA) is an entirely community-run event where mappers nominate and vote for exceptional maps and creators in a variety of categories. This year, we're thrilled to finally recognise the absolute best in every game mode (plus storyboarding)!

This post will showcase the Grand Award winners, but [every category's results can be found on the MCA website](https://mca.corsace.io/2024/results) site — don't skip the full breakdown!

## Rewards

The winners presented here will find themselves with the following rewards:

- **osu!supporter**
  - Grand Award winners: 4 months
- **Badges**
  - Winners of the Grand categories will be awarded special badges each.
  - 2nd and 3rd place of the Grand categories will be awarded a top 3 badge.

Due to comparatively low participation this year, the amount of badge awards has been reduced to only Grand category winners.
