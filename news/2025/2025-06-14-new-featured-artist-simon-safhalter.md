**Simon Safhalter** slips into our Featured Artist library!

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/481/header.jpg)

The COVID-19 pandemic led people into their bedrooms for years. While many of you passed the time clicking circles, **Simon Safhalter** tried to tackle music production!

From anime music to video game soundtracks and film scores, **Simon Safhalter** draws inspiration from widely varying sources and melds them into his own specific sound.

**10** tunes can be found on [**Simon Safhalter**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/481), including his claim to fame here on osu!: sped-up versions of his own tracks noted as "Hyper".

There's only one way to truly understand **Simon Safhalter**'s music: by playing all the maps below.
