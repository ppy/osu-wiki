## Match highlights

### ::{ flag=IT }:: Italy vs. ::{ flag=CN }:: China

**[View the match information here!](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/matches/120924693)**

    <iframe width="95%" style="aspect-ratio: 16 / 9;" src="https://player.twitch.tv/?autoplay=false&parent=osu.ppy.sh&video=2746243641" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="true" scrolling="no"></iframe>

TWC 2026's lower bracket has been producing classics for weeks now. But on Sunday, a pair of teams met in what may be the match of the tournament. Italy, the **last European team** standing and fresh off a 6–4 win against the United Kingdom, faced longtime powerhouse China after their 6–0 win against Australia, the last spot in Finals being contested by adjacent seeds.

China won the roll, and led with a strong pick of [pa-o-mu99999 - Bobobo-bo bo-bobo](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534673#taiko/5604911) (NM4), holding their pick with ease. Italy proceeded to pick [Xyris - Terrablazer](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534374#taiko/5604173) (HD2) and blow away every other score of the weekend, including China and even some winner's bracket teams, with a combined 1x100 double FC! China's next pick of [Skybreak - NOLIGHT (fear. HeyBela)](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534739#taiko/5605025) (NM2) was the shakiest so far, with Italy spending the entire first half in front before China came back to win by just over 20,000. Italy picked [MetaHumanBoi - Illustrious Drifters](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534393#taiko/5604205) (FM1) and gained the mod advantage with two HDHR plays.

That mod advantage turned out crucial, as Italy won despite their only one FC to China's two! Now at a score of 2–2, China continued their spree of tech-based picks by holding on through [Camellia - Maze of Vignere Square](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534390#taiko/5604200) (DT1), before Italy picked [Toromaru - Erinyes](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534384#taiko/5604192) (HR1), where we would learn that Italy's [ndrrr](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/4609767) was having a very, very good day.

Despite a rare bit of shaky play from Italy's star player and **the strongest player in Europe** ::{ flag=IT }:: [A-40](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/14510301), Italy won this map by 5,890 on the back of ::{ flag=IT }:: [ndrrr](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/4609767)'s 99.47% FC, sitting atop the lobby by over 30,000 score and clutching another held pick for his team. After China won [Riya - IMPULSE](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534380#taiko/5604182) (HD1) by a huge margin of over 250,000, Italy went back to the HR well, choosing [MITCH DOWNVELL - not sakura](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534381#taiko/5604184) (HR2). Due to Italy's sheer strength on HR accuracy, ::{ flag=CN }:: [kknegative](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/2349769) and ::{ flag=CN }:: [WLYMinato](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/12703319)'s double FC wasn't enough despite a pair of Italian misses, and the match reached 4–4, essentially becoming a best-of-3 with a ticket to the Finals on the line. Each team would get to pick just one more map.

China's choice was [BlackY - ULT!MATE END](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534370#taiko/5604161) (NM3), opting for the speed No Mod pick against a relatively weak high-speed team in Italy. This was also the only map they could use to get the dominant ::{ flag=IT }:: [A-40](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/14510301) out of the lobby, and forcing ::{ flag=IT }:: [ndrrr](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/4609767) to play speed was a good way to get a low score out of Italy... on any other day, that might have been true.

With an incredible performance, completely defying what people expected of his skill set, ::{ flag=IT }:: [ndrrr](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/4609767) blew through NM3 to bring his team exactly one chance at ending it before a tiebreaker. Italy called a 2-minute timeout before picking [seatrus - TEMP3ST](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534351#taiko/5604104) (NM1). This was the map Italy destroyed against the UK to claim their lone break point on Saturday, with a first-match score of 1,980,510 that would've easily been enough to beat China's Saturday performance by over 50,000 and secure victory, but this wasn’t Saturday anymore.

While China held a similar score to their first match, Italy wasn't so lucky, with the brutal ending swinging the lead over to China in the dying moments. We were headed to our **first tiebreaker since the Round of 16**.

An osu!taiko map of **[MWC 7K 2026](/wiki/Tournaments/MWC/2026_7K)'s Grand Finals TB**, [Laur - SEV-26](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534398#taiko/5604215), was all that could separate these two titans now. Nerves ran higher than ever, everyone seemed to miss on things they usually wouldn't, Italy held a slim lead for most of the first half, but eventually, China pulled away and wouldn't look back. With a brilliant recovery in the harder second half from China's strongest player, ::{ flag=CN }:: [superSSS](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/4315477), the ticket to Finals was punched in China's name, and Europe's last were eliminated from the osu!taiko World Cup. Italy go out in 7–8th place, after valiant efforts against many insanely strong teams, but China moves on to Finals to face a terrifying South Korea, fresh off a loss to Japan.

## Score highlights

- The defending champions of the last 13 years, **Japan (#1)**, have looked relatively mortal so far this tournament, dropping an uncharacteristic 3 points across their first two matches leading into a [heavyweight bout](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/2745512622) with **South Korea (#5)**, but this week they proved that any rumours of their downfall have been greatly exaggerated. In a 6–1 win with 2 players on each map, Japan scored an unreal 14 out of 14 full combos, with only one score, one of two HDHR plays on the Anti Mod pick [Aiyru - Elevator](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534394#taiko/5604206) (FM2), even dipping below 99% accuracy!
- The other [winner's bracket Semifinals match](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/2745993870) between **Chile (#2)** and **Brazil (#3)** produced a week's worth of highlight reel moments on its own, from the Chilean mainstay ::{ flag=CL }:: [ulko](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/1263669) entering the match with a pair of SS plays on [Xyris - Terrablazer](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534374#taiko/5604173) (HD2) and [Fallujah - Carved From Stone (2024 Remaster)](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534385#taiko/5604193) (NM5), to Brazilian legend ::{ flag=BR }:: [HiroK](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/4050738)'s 99.82% HDHR FC on [fool - TO THE NEXT](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534404#taiko/5604231) (FM3), and even a tiny win with a margin of 301 on [Aiobahn feat. KOTOKO - INTERNET YAMERO](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534395#taiko/5604207) (DT2). Even after the match, there was even a 99.8%, 14x100 FC from ::{ flag=BR }:: [miwoo](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/12630336) on [Laur - SEV-26](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2534398#taiko/5604215) (TB) as it was played just for fun! At 6–3 for Chile, guaranteeing their first-ever podium finish with this win, it wasn't quite as close as Italy vs. China, but it's still worth a watch before the Finals get underway.
