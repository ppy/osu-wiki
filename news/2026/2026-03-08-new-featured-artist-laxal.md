osu!'s music library wouldn't be complete without **LaXal**.

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/532/header.jpg)

Specialising in the happy hardcore genre, it should be no surprise that **LaXal** draws inspiration from artists like [*S3RL*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/9), [*Stonebank*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/254), *Sub Zero Project*, *Da Tweekaz*, and many more!

Over the years, **LaXal** has performed live sets in Australia, Japan, the United States, the Netherlands, Spain, Portugal, and France. With his inclusion in osu!, one could argue that he has reached everywhere else in the world too.

**5** of **LaXal**'s ultra-catchy tunes are now part of [his Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/532), pre-timed just for you.

Preview some tracks, then start a map of your own:

### LaXal feat. Melissa Brooks - Horizons

Two of **LaXal**'s biggest passions are music and video games. If you played [this osu!catch map](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2478800) by [mieldeprairie](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/19290397), you'd understand how those passions intertwine.
