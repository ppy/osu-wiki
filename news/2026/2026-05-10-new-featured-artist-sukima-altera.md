The intersection of rhythm games and electronic dance music is now part of our Featured Artists!

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/542/header.jpg)

Hailing from the Philippines, freelance producer **Sukima Altera** (known in the VTuber world as *SeiiYuumi*) is a specialist in the indie EDM scene. Since launching his musical career in 2020, **Sukima Altera** has been exploring opportunities across the spectrum, ranging from live performances at JAPANIGHT and launching his own music circle *YOKAICORE*, to features in compilation albums and various rhythm games like *vivid/stasis*, *EX-XDRiVER* and *OpenTaiko*.

**Sukima Altera** also draws inspiration from rhythm game greats like [*Yooh*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/218), [*BlackY*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/150), *Yu-dachi*, *BCM*, [*Massive New Krew*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/318) and many more, so you can rest assured knowing that **Sukima Altera** is crafting hardcore tracks that can be appreciated by anyone in the rhythm game scene.

"Anyone in the rhythm game scene" includes osu! players too. That's why you have the choice of **16** different songs from [**Sukima Altera**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/542)!

### Sukima Altera - INDUSTRY SLAYER

*BMS of Fighters* is an annual contest for the doujin music scene. **Sukima Altera** debuted in the contest with his track *INDUSTRY SLAYER*, which also happens to be the track **Sukima Altera** debuted with [in osu!'s Ranked section](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2472648) thanks to [Wispy](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/11106929)!
