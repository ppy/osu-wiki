[Resurrection Cup](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/2205168), or ResCup for short, is an annual open-rank 3v3, team size 6 tournament that seeks to highlight the pinnacle of the osu!standard tournament scene.

## What's new?

In line with this year's theme, "Codename: Genesis", the ResCup team has reoriented its philosophy towards the "genesis" of tournament success – the players. Hence, the 5th iteration of ResCup brings drastic changes to its internal management.

Not to worry, ResCup will still deliver on its yearly promise to provide high-level production for its players, but the way in which the means reach the ends has been heavily reworked. These include tighter controls on quality across all fronts — originals, mapping, design and broadcast — a more streamlined development and quality assurance cycle, and a player-centric pooling philosophy.

As such, we have heavily cut back our originals count this year to 18, essentially halving the scale at which we operated at from last year. This change has allowed us to more efficiently allocate resources, alleviating the burden on poolers and designers and allowing each original to shine just a little bit brighter.

In short, as this year's ResCup takes a step back from previous iterations in terms of scale, it takes two steps forward in terms of efficiency and quality while keeping what truly matters at heart. Take this news post as our hope that this ResCup will top its predecessors in terms of quality over quantity.

## How do I register?

To register, grab at least 3 friends and fill out the [registration form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdxGhsO75C87lybcdrjtYsYChhmxRmPRxtaWCe321n1eFmBLw/viewform) to sign up as a team! Before registering, please make sure that:

- You have not have violated the [osu! community rules](/wiki/Rules) within the past 12 months.
- You have joined the [ResCup26 Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/UNzyfgGfeu).

We also encourage spectators to join the [Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/UNzyfgGfeu) in order to keep up with important announcements and livestream notifications. For more information, check out the [forum post](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/2205168) and [main sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1Md62htYhsmBaVn8eM29XJBgaFCbmvcLJyqdcu5k9Gwo).
