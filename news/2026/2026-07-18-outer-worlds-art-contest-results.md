The summer is in full reason and new artworks are ready to greet it!

![](/wiki/shared/news/2026-07-18-outer-worlds-art-contest-results/banner.jpg)

With **30,376** votes cast from **2,529** individual players, the results of our humble drawing competition are ready to be presented.

## Finalists

::: alert-tip
**Tip**
You can click on any of the banners to view the full image.
:::

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/Triantafyllia.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/Triantafyllia.png)

An astronomic **1,206 stargazers** were captured by Alisa's silver swirls and starry sheens in **[Triantafyllia](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/17084594)** radiant artwork, exploding it like quasar rays into a victorious lead of 130 votes, clinching the premiere spot of the winners' spotlight!

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/PEALEERD%5FTAK.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/PEALEERD_TAK.jpg)

**[PEALEERD_TAK](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/6214002)** reached deep into outer space with **1,076** silent observers beholding an eerie wreckage vista coloured with Alisa's signature blue visage, landing elegantly in a welcome silver finish!

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/Bayki.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/Bayki.png)

**[Bayki](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/15984389)** managed to snag smirks from **957** space cadets who related with the experience of being thrown spinning when stepping out on a space walk. The world if tough out there, but some of the lost footing clearly bounced back to net the entry third place and another new face on our art contest podium!

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/Dreamxiety.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/Dreamxiety.png)

**[Dreamxiety](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/13103233)** showed us a picture of pippi in orbit along with alien friends and her trusty Rio keychain, composed beatifully against the backdrop of the Blue A— *couch*, Blue Marble, reaching **930** favourable impressions back home.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/LeeNarie.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/LeeNarie.jpg)

**[LeeNarie](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/2667849)** gave Mocha and pippi tickets to board the Taikonator Express, but oh no, our clumsy pippi has dropped them! Mocha managed to snap a neat photo while the train approached, however, impressing **875** netizens.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/h3p0.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/h3p0.jpg)

**[h3p0](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/15875092)** rolled the jackpot with **777** votes on their composition of pippi, Mocha and Don. Slot machine noises were heard from across the galaxy as osu! fans recognised several of the references scattered throughout the piece.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/Porukana.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/Porukana.png)

**[Porukana](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/12992775)**'s pippi found herself in a precarious situation while on a trip to low-Earth orbit. About third of the **697** online bystanders cheered her on for until the repair crew arrived, some praised the incredible lighting and composition of the picture, and a few started unexplainedly pointing with one finger.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/puykka.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/puykka.png)

**[puykka](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/38985575)**'s pastel pink landscape image featuring Don adorably looking at an otherworldly sky invited **666** curious pairs of eyes to stare right back from the other side of the sky. They say the wordless stare-off continued for hours.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/MinMin%5F9s.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/MinMin_9s.png)

**[MinMin_9s](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/7140649)** found a space-walking Mocha along with an acrobatic Aiko and a cutely menacing Don floating on the other side of their spaceship window. **631** onlookers couldn't help but watch the shenanigans unfold.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/X%2Dill.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/X-ill.png)

**[X-ill](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/21771922)**'s melancholic scene of Alisa meeting upon a dying star and taking off her helmet in ineffable awe caught **626** astronauts right by the heartstrings and inflicted at least half as many cases of inscrutable wonder.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/KotobaT.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/KotobaT.png)

**[KotobaT](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/38854827)** designed a snappy poster for pippi's space adventure, with friends who are all *totally* trustworthy. With approval ratings numbering **553** and such a confident smile, we're pleased to entrust this pippi with main menu privileges and first dibs on osu!supporter perks.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/Sey10.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/Sey10.png)

**[Sey10](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/5843837)**'s pippi managed to find a world the size of her palm, stunned in wornder at the possibility of playing mini golf on a cosmic scale... probably. **535** humans were stuck hoping she doesn't happen upon their own wet mud ball.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/hurbruh.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/hurbruh.png)

**[hurbruh](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/18537231)** posed a simple question: who would win in an orbital jetpack fight? Half of the mission control staff urged them to stop, the rest were already placing their bets and **518** live viewers were waiting for the next slide of the transmission broadcast.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/serr.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/serr.png)

**[serr](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/17858303)** entered with an artwork of pippi holding an orb of light casting shadows of thin-film interference patterns, wowing **512** physicists with the discovery of such an impossibily beautiful deep-space phenomenon.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/soncak.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/soncak.png)

**[soncak](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/3866821)**'s pixel art rendition of pippi about to snap a black hole in half struck pure fear into **508** mortals who promptly hoisted the entry into the winners' circle where it belongs.

## ppy picks

As is tradition, osu!'s lead developer ::{ flag=AU }:: [peppy](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/2) has hand-picked his favourite entries outside the usual top 15 from the community vote and popped them into the main menu among the finalists.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/%2D%20Y%20u%20m%20i%20J%20i%2D.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/-%20Y%20u%20m%20i%20J%20i-.jpg)

**[- Y u m i J i-](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/7656048)** sent an image of a chibi pippi and chibi Mocha chilling happily around a star system. For all we know, they could be huge and very far away, but when we punch the trig distance functions into a calculator with that amusing thought in mind, it too simply makes a happy face.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/elawa.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/elawa.png)

**[elawa](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/20331332)** instantly knew how to turn up Alisa's midnight motif to the extreme. The vibrant colours couldn't be missed from anywhere within the local supercluster, and neither could we ignore it with our puny eyes.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/Berinjela%20Chan.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/Berinjela%20Chan.png)

**[Berinjela Chan](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/14662944)** sent the osu! crew on a trip through a black hole, featuring cute asteroidal easter eggs, chromatic colouration and imminent spaghettification, indicatively a causation of unstoppably prolonged elongated articulation — requesting immediate penmanship restoration following unelaborately conclusive exhalation—

Ah.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/YellowX21.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/YellowX21.jpg)

**[YellowX21](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/16264297)** referred pippi to Don's space taxi tour around the osu! cookie globe. It was 'nuff said and we're jealous to have missed the ride.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/Seras56.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/Seras56.png)

**[Seras56](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/37860508)** along with pippi and Mocha started the continued space tour, somehow convincing peppy to allocate main menu ad space for it. Of course, the exorbitant pricing will directly help fund peppy's future space cruiser.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/acolta.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/acolta.png)

**[acolta](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/19254220)** sent us newly graduated space caded pippi's selfie from orbit! Technically, depending on where you live, you'd be photo-bombing the picture.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/Shiratori%2DP.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/Shiratori-P.jpeg)

**[Shiratori-P](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/13827317)** made us behold a planet far away, with alternate versions of our familiar crew on a mission to become osu! rhythm champions. This one exudes creativity and imagination.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/banners/ProMovesCS.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/285/winners/ProMovesCS.png)

**[ProMovesCS](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/35619026)**'s pippi and Mocha had a bit of a situation near Earth frantically struggling to resolve it, making for an endearing entry with a sort of emotional expression you don't see everywhere.
