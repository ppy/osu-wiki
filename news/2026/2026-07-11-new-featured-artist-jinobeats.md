Nothing can stop **JinoBeats** from infiltrating our Featured Artist library.

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/477/header.jpg)

Going by different aliases depending on genre, American DTM producer **JinoBeats** (a.k.a. *Xeveir and Xeron*, *Jouxl Eterna*, *TeKno.loji* and probably more names) is an aural storyteller, connecting lore between their tracks from their personally crafted realms of the Seraphim to the Aetherial Wars and onwards. The more you listen to their songs, the more you'll understand how they're connected.

[A Featured Artist page for **JinoBeats**](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/477) has existed since [Resonance of Ice Stalactites](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2373140) debuted in [Lazer Grand Arena 2025](/wiki/Tournaments/LGA/2025), but today that listing is growing more than tenfold.

A grand total of **19** tracks are now on [**JinoBeats**' listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/477), all available for mapping until the heat death of the universe (or osu!'s servers, whichever comes first).

If you're not sure where to start, preview the stories **JinoBeats** has crafted below.

If you happen to be involved in [this year's osu!taiko Mapping World Cup](https://osu.ppy.sh/home/news/2026-06-18-osutaiko-mapping-world-cup-2026-registrations-open), you'll be listening to a lot of **JinoBeats** starting today. The tournament's first mapping phase requires participants to create a map using **any of [JinoBeats' featured songs](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/477)**, so best of luck deciding which masterpiece to focus on.

For the rest of you, visit [**JinoBeats**' Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/477) to see everything this artist has to offer. If that's too complicated, you might have a heart attack if you open the osu! editor to design a map.
