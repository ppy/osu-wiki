Halt the pens and warp drives — it's time to prep the fireworks and bless our returning explorers!

![](/wiki/shared/news/2026-06-12-outer-worlds-art-contest/banner.jpg)

Landing at a count of **150** entries, artists from around the globe ventured into the universe and brought back unique visions of the stars. Now it's *your* turn to decide which of them captured the most vivid imaginations (and you get to pick your **15** favourites)!

To aid in acquainting yourselves with the contestants, here's a quick slideshow of the submissions to the tunes of *Exit This Earth's Atomosphere ([**Camellia**](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/31)'s "PLANETARY//200STEP" Remix)*:

The voting phase will run for **7 days** from the date of this post and will end on **July 16 @ 18:00 UTC**. The winners receive the following rewards:

- A selection of the top-voted finalists will be **featured on the main menu** and receive **3 months of osu!supporter**.
- The top 3 will receive bespoke **profile badges** made from their own creations.
- The top 4 will also be featured as [**limited-time default profile banners**](https://osu.ppy.sh/home/news/2024-07-15-aerial-antics-art-results#new-profile-customisations), with the ones from last round rotating out.

As always, we can't thank all of you artists enough for participating with your time and effort — we wouldn't be here without you!

Enough ceremony. Go pick your favourite artwork!
