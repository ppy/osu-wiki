We're kicking off the year with **70** new songs from artists across our Featured Artist library!

![](/wiki/shared/news/banners/featured-artist.jpg)

For anyone out of the loop, osu! has an [official music collection](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists) where osu! players (such as *you*) can download pre-timed beatmap templates of over **10,000** songs from over **500** artists, with more being added every weekend.

This week's music addition is far from the norm. Instead of adding tracks from a single artist, we're pulling songs **16** different artists into our Featured Artist library! Each of these artists are part of the osu! community already, so you can think of these as expansions to their current listings.

For extra clarity, we've marked every new track with a highly conspicuous **NEW** icon.

![](/wiki/shared/news/2026-01-10-featured-artist-track-updates-winter-2026/new.png)

Click on any artist's banner to head to their full music listing, where you can download pre-timed `.osz` files for their music! Use these links to skip to see what's new for a specific artist:
