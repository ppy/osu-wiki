Attention new mappers! The annual beatmapping contest exclusively for novice mappers - **Newspaper Cup** - is open for submission again now!

![](https://regraz.s-ul.eu/a4Az6ecA)

The Newspaper Cup is originally a Chinese mapping contest to promote novice mappers, which had its first iteration in 2015. Through the past years, the Newspaper Cup has become a renowned contest for encouraging fresh blood into mapping and finding new talents across the entire mapping community, and it will always be the core value of Newspaper Cup.

Newspaper Cup 2026, the 12th occurrence of this annual event, will be hosted by ::{ flag=CN }:: [Mafumafu](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/3076909), and ::{ flag=CN }:: [Atrue](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/1758523). We welcome and strongly encourage novice mappers to participate!

Please join [our official Discord group](https://discord.gg/fVgU9pA) for the latest updates. For Chinese contestants: [点击此处查看中文版主题。](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/2169198?n=1)

## Rules

Before you start working on your submission, please make sure you familiarise yourself with the rules below.

* This contest is for the **osu! mode only**.
* You must submit through the [Newspaper Cup 2026 Page in the Contest Listing]()
* Your submission must be a `.osz` file exported from the osu! in-game editor. You must submit at the designated submission site.
* Your submission should comply with the [Ranking Criteria](https://osu.ppy.sh/wiki/en/Ranking_criteria), except for those that cannot apply (e.g., spread requirements).
* Your submission should **not contain information that reveals your identity**.
* You must complete your submission independently. **Do not upload or share** your submission with others before the results announcement.
* Beatmaps made by **generative artificial intelligence are not allowed**.
* You are allowed to use **custom hitsounds and custom combo colors**.
  However, you are **not allowed** to add backgrounds, videos, or storyboards.
* **You are required to finish one and only one difficulty** in your submission.

Additionally, **NPC 2026 is exclusive for novice mappers.** You must satisfy all of the following eligibility criteria:

* You must have joined osu! **before 2025-12-01**.
* You are **not a winner** of other osu! mode mapping contests (e.g., PDC, NPC, MBC, oBWC, etc.).
  *(Cut-off date: 2025-12-15)*
* You have **never been a member** of mapping-related groups (NAT, QAT, BAT, MAT, BN, etc.) in the osu! mode.
* The total drain time of your ranked [Insane (and above) difficulties](https://osu.ppy.sh/wiki/en/Beatmap/Difficulty) **before 2025-12-15** must be **below 5 minutes**.
  Collab difficulties are counted by corresponding sections mapped.
* Throughout the contest, the hosts may inspect if you qualify for novice status.

## Songs

There will be **two tracks** in NPC 2026:

* **Featured Artist Track**
  You are free to choose any track listed in the [Featured Artist Library](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists), **except osu!originals** (as osu!original has a standalone track). *Reasonable cutting is allowed.*

* **osu!original Track**
  You are free to choose any track listed in the Featured Artist Library that are tagged as [osu!originals](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/tracks?exclusive_only=1). *Cutting is **NOT allowed** for this track.*

## Judging

### Judging Criteria

* **Expertise (50%)**
  How well the map reflects the music elements, with emphasis on music reflection, pattern/rhythm consistency, coherence, and playability.
  As NPC is for novice mappers, judges will focus more on **solid fundamental execution** as the primary objective.

* **Creativity (35%)**
  How the map stands out compared to current mapping trends and other submissions.
  Generic maps or copy-paste concepts are discouraged. Creativity built on top of solid expertise is highly valued.

* **Judge’s Impression (15%)**
  Judges may award or deduct points based on noteworthy aspects, such as outstanding sections, hitsounds, or notable weaknesses.

Judging scores will be **standardized** and submissions are **anonymous** to judges.
