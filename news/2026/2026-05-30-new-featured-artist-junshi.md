Hip-hop and anime give off polar opposite impressions, yet our latest Featured Artist combines both as if it's natural.

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/xxx/header.jpg)

A master of his own alternative anime-influenced rap music, **Junshi** has been making a name for himself in the underground hip-hop scene since 2016.

His first breakthrough track "Twilight 1995" found its way to hundreds of thousands of individuals' ears, kickstarting **Junshi**'s evolution into cloud rap and hyperpop, sprinkling in more anime and visual novel influences throughout his own emotional style.

If you're already convinced (which you should be), you'll find **6** tracks on [**Junshi**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/546), each available in a pre-timed beatmap template. That means you can load it in the editor and start mapping on frame 1.

Use your precise clicking skills to click on the "play" button for all of these previews:
