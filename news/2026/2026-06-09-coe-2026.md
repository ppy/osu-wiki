The world's osu! players are gathering at the yearly summit. Are you in?

![](/wiki/share/news/2026-06-09-coe-2026/banner.jpg)

For those new to the scene, **cavoe's osu! event (*COE*)** is the world's largest osu! gathering, bringing together thousands of rhythm game enthusiasts at **Brabanthallen in Den Bosch, Netherlands**.

Remember COE 2025? Its main event, the annual COE tournament drew in a spectacle that highlights what makes LAN events so memorable. The competition was underscored by the most competitive lineups in the event's history, featuring many of the world's elite players.

In a surprising twist of the bracket, the two finalists from 2023 faced off on the very first day of the 2025 tournament, delivering a "grand final" atmosphere right from the opening round. The last tournament day culminated in a highly anticipated final between global leaderboard titan [mrekk](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/7562902) and the reigning tournament champion, [MALISZEWSKI](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/12408961).

The event also featured a variety of side activities, including a "pp race" event where all COE visitors collectively challenged [mrekk](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/7562902), as well as the swan song iteration of the [GTS tournament series](https://osu.ppy.sh/home/news/2025-11-20-global-taiko-showdown-ending). If you're thinking of getting ready for this year's IRL competitions or even just meeting new friends, then read on for more details!

### 1v1 osu! tournament

![](/wiki/share/news/2026-06-09-coe-2026/main-stage.jpg)

As with every year, we are hosting the COE 1v1 osu! tournament — striving to top off even last year's peak moments. Featuring an additional mappool and a double-elimination bracket, there's sure to be many a close match. Will anyone be able to claim the throne after [mrekk](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/7562902)'s dominant performance last year? Be ready for some of osu!'s biggest names to battle it out on stage!

If you are one of those big names (or aspire to be!) then you should know that the registrations for this tournament are live! Check [the registration page](https://sign-up.cavoe.events/) for all the information you need to know!

Once the tournament is underway, catch it live on [Twitch](https://twitch.tv/coevent) and [YouTube](https://youtube.com/@COEvent).

### Side events

![](/wiki/share/news/2026-06-09-coe-2026/side-events.jpg)

Apart from the big tournament, this time there will be a number of additional tournaments:

- An osu! mode 1v1 tournament for all COE26 visitors to participate in
- COE's first-ever osu!mania tounament
- A *Chunithm* tournament, also a first for COE
- A *Counter-Strike 2* Wingman tournament, returning after last year's success
- YEAST ([YokesPai](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/6399568)'s Extravagant Accuracy Sight Read Tournament)

Catch more info on these tournaments including registrations when available on the [COE Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/9pZWjF5ZnK).

Of course, there won't be just tournaments. Here are some of the additional things in store:

- Group dinner at a local sushi restaurant
- An unforgettable day at the Efteling theme park
- ...Many more things yet to be announced!

### On-site activities

![](/wiki/share/news/2026-06-09-coe-2026/activities.jpg)

There will of course be tons of miscellanous things to do at the venue, even while all the crazy big events are going on:

- An arcade right at the venue featuring [Pixel Arcade](https://pixelarcade.nl/)'s cabinets, packed with a variety of rhythm games. Free for every visitor!
- A VR gaming booth for you *Beat Saber* enthusiasts
- Table tennis tournaments and board game area for between-match fun
- Tons of venue space to meet fellow players and make new friends

Looking to join the fun? You'll find information about tickets on the [COE website](https://cavoe.events/)!
