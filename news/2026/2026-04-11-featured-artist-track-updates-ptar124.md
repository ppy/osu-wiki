New songs are added to osu!'s Featured Artist library each week. That's awesome already, but songs produced [specifically for osu!](/wiki/Community/Bespoke_music) are even more awesome, and **ptar124** is adding a bunch to his Featured Artist catalogue today!

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/409/header.jpg)

[**ptar124**](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/409) joined our Featured Artists [a bit over two years ago](https://osu.ppy.sh/home/news/2024-03-31-new-featured-artist-ptar124). Since then, he's been pouring new music into the osu! community's ears nonstop, culminating in [his debut album](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4BPb70Pdk-s) packed almost entirely with music designed for tournaments throughout the last few years!

[**ptar124**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/409) has **9** new tracks to choose from, but that's only the tip of the iceberg. Almost all of the songs that were already available on **ptar124**'s listing have been remastered by [rN](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/190), so you can now map them in their freshest form!

In total, there are **16 new/updated tracks** on [**ptar124**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/409), each available in full pre-timed `.osz` glory. 

See some of **ptar124**'s newly added [osu! originals](/wiki/Community/Bespoke_music) in action below, then use them as inspiration for your own maps!
