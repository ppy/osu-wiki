It's time.

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/534/header.jpg)

Starting in 2003, online rhythm game *O2Jam* began releasing music from a composer under the alias of **Brandy**. While details of their musical identity beyond that alias are unknown, **Brandy**'s music has nonetheless made an impact in the internet space for two decades and counting.

In the most literal sense, **Brandy** has been here on osu! since the beginning. On October 7, 2007, beatmap submission became available to the osu! community, and a beatmap of [Love Fighter](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/16) was published (osu!'s 16th submitted beatmap, if the URL is to be believed!), making **Brandy** osu!'s most historic Featured Artist to date.

Enough rambling. There are **6** pre-timed `.osz` files on [**Brandy**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/534). Dive into some classic maps and create your own new-school renditions:

### Brandy - Cross Time !!

Often regarded as "ahead of its time" (despite using Approach Rate 4), [this 2007 map showcased in the video below](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/85) by [FFFanatic](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/677) is essential reading for any osu! player.

Once you're done with that, try one of these too:

- [Another older-generation map](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/5838) hosted by [Crazy Jay](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/44867)
- [Ancient Loved mega-set](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/5918) hosted by [machol30](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/5772) (a.k.a. *buraimaster1234*)
- [A newer interpretation](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/973162) hosted by [Leader](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/631530)
- [Obligatory osu!taiko map](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/933765) hosted by [Konpaku Sariel](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/533502)
- [4K osu!mania map](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/886126) hosted by [ababab0306](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/4715408)
