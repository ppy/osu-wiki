An Australian origin isn't the only thing osu! and our newest Featured Artist have in common.

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/538/header.jpg)

**jeko** (known around here as [J3C](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/15956540)) has been clicking circles for the better half of a decade, where they've been hearing the rhythm game-y sounds of renowned producers like [*Camellia*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/31), *celtix* and *ReeK*.

Consistent exposure is bound to have an effect. In the words of **jeko**...

> osu! is the biggest cause for my music style

Through scattered tournament songs, presence in the Ranked section, and official inclusion in our Featured Artist catalogue as of today, **jeko**'s osu!-inspired sound is now ready to inspire the rhythm game soundscape's next generation.

To be more specific, **17** tracks are officially part of [**jeko**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/538)! That means at a moment's notice, you can pick up a pre-timed `.osz` and make something cool (or just *something* if you've never opened the editor before).

Regardless of how you engage with **jeko**'s music, we can all agree that it's awesome. Listen to some previews below if you haven't already:
