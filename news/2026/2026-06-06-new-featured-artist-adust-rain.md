Extra Stage has been selected.

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/548/header.jpg)

Japan-based music project **Adust Rain** is known for what they call "heavy pop": the fusion of heavy metal instrumentation and catchy pop vocals. For over 10 years, they've been producing their own emotionally dramatic arrangements of classic Touhou tracks, and later branched out into their own fully original works!

osu! is no stranger to Touhou music, so it's no surprise that **Adust Rain** has been popular among circle clickers since 2019.

On top of that, **Adust Rain** has gained a reputation on osu! for their music's sheer intensity. [*psychology*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/890981) and its nearly 8-star difficulty was osu!'s first introduction to the band, and their latest Ranked map [*Joker's Cranberrybox*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/1815728) showcases a spread with *8* difficulties above the 6-star barrier. At the peak, [*TRAUMATIC SYNDROME -Lenboxx Remix-*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/1064743) tops out at over 9 stars.

And yes, this means anyone rated near the peak of osu!(lazer)'s new [*Ranked Play*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FdAS8LHID9g) mode will be hearing a lot more of **Adust Rain**'s music.

For the rest of us, [**Adust Rain**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/548) has pre-timed beatmap templates for **15** different tracks. If you're looking to create something truly challenging, the canvas is yours.
