We don't need to describe this artist's music. His alias already does.

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/545/header.jpg)

Just like you, **Dazzling** (previously known as *ez2dal*) is part of the rhythm game community. While *your* experience may only be within osu! though, **Dazzling** has spread throughout the rhythm game scene, especially known for his contributions to games like *A Dance of Fire and Ice*. As a rhythm game map/chart creator himself, **Dazzling** knows what goes into musical-focused level design, and is able to capture those nuances in his own aural expertise.

Here on osu!, **Dazzling** reached the Ranked category in early 2026 with [a couple back-to-back maps](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets?q=ranked%3C2026-05-23%20artist%3D%22dazzling%22). His fate in osu! from here onwards is in the hands of mappers.

Make your way to [**Dazzling**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/545) to grab any of the **3** pre-timed beatmap templates for these tunes.
