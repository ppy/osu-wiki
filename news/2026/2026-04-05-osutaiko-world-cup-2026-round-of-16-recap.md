## Match highlights

### ::{ flag=CO }:: Colombia vs. ::{ flag=VN }:: Vietnam

**[View the match information here!](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/matches/120825509)**

<div align="center" class="osu-md__paragraph">
    <iframe width="95%" style="aspect-ratio: 16 / 9;" src="https://player.twitch.tv/?video=2734858509&parent=osu.ppy.sh&autoplay=false" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="true" scrolling="no"></iframe>
</div>

Standing as the closest match of this TWC's round of 16, Colombia (#30) and Vietnam (#26) have certainly put on a splendid show to hold their ground and earn one more chance to progress through the bracket.

Vietnam struck first on [XH - Corrupted Paywall](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2527046#taiko/5582863) (NM3) backed by a near-SS from ::{ flag=VN }:: [\[TCD\] Azure](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/18782185), while ::{ flag=CO }:: [Xoretra](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/4940698) fell short after a costly mid-map miss. Colombia quickly responded on [orangentle / Yu_Asahina - OEFHEBEN](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2428701#taiko/5582896) (HR2), and despite Vietnam edging ahead on  [A-One - Side by Side](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2527072#taiko/5582922) (DT2) thanks to ::{ flag=VN }:: [RandomNameIdk](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/24042710) holding the only FC in the lobby, neither side could establish control early on.

On [Kagetora. - Tenchi-Kaibyaku](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2528068#taiko/5585695) (FM2), the tug-of-war between the two teams reached its peak. Colombia surged ahead early, only for Vietnam to flip the stakes midway through the map. A critical chain miss from Vietnam in the final quarter, however, handed the advantage right back, setting up a tense closing stretch on the final chorus with frequently traded misses from both sides. In the end, Colombia stood victorious and narrowly clutched the point by a mere *7,163* score difference, keeping the match locked in a back-and-forth flow.

Fate's kindness didn't last for long, as Vietnam swiftly reclaimed control on this round's osu! original, [OLDUCT - Buy Now!](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2527043#taiko/5582858) (NM2), where the map's mixed snaps proved difficult for Colombia to manage. However, any attempt to pull ahead was immediately challenged on [sasakure.UK feat. Shirakami Fubuki - KINGWORLD](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/1784288#taiko/3654236) (NM1), yet another razor-thin battle. Colombia capitalised on an early miss to control most of the map, but as the gap tightened to almost nothing around the third quarter, Vietnam briefly stole the lead during the final buildup, only to lose it again to a pivotal miss right after. Colombia held firm to the end, despite a near-SS from ::{ flag=VN }:: [RandomNameIdk](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/24042710) keeping the outcome on a knife's edge.

Vietnam found their footing once more on [Yunomi - Game Over (feat. TORIENA)](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2527202#taiko/5583227) (DT1), but Colombia refused to go down quietly. On [seatrus - Raindrop](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2527055#taiko/5582877) (HD1) they delivered yet another clutch performance — handing over the lead early before capitalising on a string of late misses from Vietnam to reclaim it and force the match into a tiebreaker. Neither side was willing to give up an inch, neither the match or even a single break point.

Despite Colombia's incredible effort to push the match to its absolute limit, they tragically fell just short. On [Cansol - Out of Place](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2527074#taiko/5582924) (TB), Vietnam delivered under pressure, with the map's mixed snaps proving yet again too nerve-racking for Colombia. With that, Vietnam secured the win and their place in the Quarterfinals.

All in all, nothing was held back on either side. Both teams showcased relentless willpower and made the win that much more exhilarating. Congratulations to Vietnam on their victory, and commendations to Colombia for delivering a truly spectacular, hard-fought showdown, alongside an extra shout-out to ::{ flag=VN }:: [RandomNameIdk](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/24042710) for their MVP-worthy performance as a constant presence across most maps and the consistent top scorer of the lobby!
