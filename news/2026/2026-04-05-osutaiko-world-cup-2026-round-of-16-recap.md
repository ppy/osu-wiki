## Match highlights

### ::{ flag=CO }:: Colombia vs. ::{ flag=VN }:: Vietnam

**[View the match information here!](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/matches/120825509)**

<div align="center" class="osu-md__paragraph">
    <iframe width="95%" style="aspect-ratio: 16 / 9;" src="https://player.twitch.tv/?video=2734858509&parent=osu.ppy.sh&autoplay=false" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="true" scrolling="no"></iframe>
</div>

Standing as the closest match of this TWC's round of 16, Colombia (#30) and Vietnam (#26) have certainly put on a splendid show to hold their ground and earn one more chance to progress through the bracket.

Starting off the match with [XH - Corrupted Paywall](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2527046#taiko/5582863) (NM3), Vietnam scores their first point on home ground with a near-SS from ::{ flag=VN }:: [\[TCD\] Azure](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/18782185). Meanwhile, ::{ flag=CO }:: [Xoretra](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/4940698) looked set to answer back with equally stellar accuracy, but with a costly mid-map miss breaking their combo, it ultimately left Vietnam uncontested for the opener.

However, Colombia wasted no time striking back on their own pick [orangentle / Yu_Asahina - OEFHEBEN](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2428701#taiko/5582896) (HR2), putting up a solid counter play to secure their first point of the match.

The back-and-forth continued on [A-One - Side by Side](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2527072#taiko/5582922) (DT2) where Vietnam edged once more, largely thanks to an FC from ::{ flag=VN }:: [RandomNameIdk](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/24042710), being the only player to hold their nerve throughout the map.

The same energy carried into [Kagetora. - Tenchi-Kaibyaku](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2528068#taiko/5585695) (FM2), where the tug-of-war between the two teams reached its peak. Colombia opened with an early lead, only for Vietnam to wrestle control back around the halfway mark. A critical chain miss from Vietnam in the final quarter, however, handed the advantage right back, setting up a tense closing stretch on the final chorus with frequently traded misses from both sides. In the end, Colombia stood victorious and narrowly clutched the point by a mere *7,163* score difference, keeping the match locked in a back-and-forth flow.

Fate's kindness didn't last for long, as Vietnam swiftly reclaimed control on this round's osu! original, [OLDUCT - Buy Now!](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2527043#taiko/5582858) (NM2), putting up a solid performance to take back the point in style, as the map's mixed snaps proved just tricky enough to disrupt Colombia's consistency.

Still, Colombia refused to let the momentum slip away entirely, answering right back on [sasakure.UK feat. Shirakami Fubuki - KINGWORLD](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/1784288#taiko/3654236) (NM1), where another tense back-and-forth unfolded yet again. Capitalising on an early miss from Vietnam in the first quarter, they held the lead for the majority of the map, maintaining control even as the gap narrowed to razor-thin margin heading into the third quarter. Vietnam managed to edge ahead during the final buildup, but a pivotal miss right at the start of the final section handed the lead straight back. From there, Colombia held firm to secure a well-earned point, despite a near-SS performance from ::{ flag=VN }:: [RandomNameIdk](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/24042710) keeping the outcome on a knife's edge.

Vietnam found their footing once more on [Yunomi - Game Over (feat. TORIENA)](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2527202#taiko/5583227) (DT1), delivering a solid performance to secure the point with a comfortable lead, even with ::{ flag=CO }:: [Xoretra](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/4940698) holding the only FC in the lobby, it ultimately wasn't enough to turn the tide in Colombia's favour.

Colombia weren't about to let things slip away just yet, and they proved it on [seatrus - Raindrop](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2527055#taiko/5582877) (HD1) with yet another hard-fought win that — yet again — solidified the intense back-and-forth energy that defined this whole match. They opened with the early lead, only for Vietnam to quickly wrest control away. Things looked solid for them for a while, until a heavy string of misses from their end around the final buildup allowed Colombia to seize the lead back once more. From there, they held on until the finish line to secure the point, reinforcing the match's restless energy as neither side was willing to give up an inch.

Despite taking Vietnam to the very end through a fierce and valiant battle, their miracle run was tragically cut short on the tiebreaker, as the momentum simply wasn't on their side for the final push, especially with mixed snaps not being their forte as seen through their performance in NM2. With that, Vietnam closed out the match on [Cansol - Out of Place](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/2527074#taiko/5582924) (TB) with a commanding performance, earning their passage towards the tournament's Quarterfinals against Russia.

All in all, neither Vietnam nor Colombia have held back any punches during this match. Both sides showcased relentless willpower and made the win that much more exhilarating. We extend a huge congratulations to Vietnam on their win, and commend Colombia for delivering a truly spectacular, hard-fought showdown. We'd also like to give an extra shoutout to ::{ flag=VN }:: [RandomNameIdk](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/24042710) for their MVP-worthy performance as a constant presence across most maps and the consistent top scorer of the lobby!
