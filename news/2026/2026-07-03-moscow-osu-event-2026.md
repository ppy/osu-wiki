The fourth iteration of the largest osu! event in Russia is coming later this month, 25–26th of July!

![](/wiki/shared/news/2026-07-03-moscow-osu-event-2026/banner.jpg)

[See the Russian version of this article here! Русскоязычную версию новости можно прочитать здесь!](https://play-osu.ru/?p=187)

This year's Moscow osu! Event (MOE) will once again take place at Mosproducer, a concert hall situated at the centre of Moscow, Russia.

If you are unfamiliar with the Russia's biggest osu! event, take a look at some of the things awaiting you:

### Tournaments

![Tournament players](/wiki/shared/news/2026-07-03-moscow-osu-event-2026/tournament_players.jpg)

We have tournaments for all osu! game modes, with osu!mania having separate tournaments for 4K and 7K players.

The main game mode tournament has already went through both the open and closed qualifiers, with the following players taking guaranteed slots in the bracket: ::{ flag=BY }:: [twennity](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/15803625), ::{ flag=RU }:: [villix](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/12739835), ::{ flag=RU }:: [Hober38](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/19887342), ::{ flag=RU }:: [shadevr](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/8165685), ::{ flag=RU }:: [GodRoPoNiKa](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/11195861), ::{ flag=RU }:: [fedotoff](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/7351448), ::{ flag=RU }:: [Antaanar](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/13206785) and ::{ flag=RU }:: [My Angel Kita](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/21101642). With 8 more directly invited players to be announced soon, a total of 16 participants will compete against each other on stage!

For the other game modes, only the final matches will be played at the event itself in a free-for-all 1x1x1x1 format, with players getting on stage via online qualifiers.

### Cosplay show

![Cosplay show](/wiki/shared/news/2026-07-03-moscow-osu-event-2026/cosplay.jpg)

As a tradition, a cosplay show will be held at the event, allowing anyone to express themselves as their favourite character. Feel free to [check out last year's recap](https://osu.ppy.sh/home/news/2025-10-08-moscow-osu-event-2025-recap#cosplay-show) to find out what to expect!

### Gaming booth

![Gaming booth](/wiki/shared/news/2026-07-03-moscow-osu-event-2026/gaming_booth.jpg)

A dedicated gaming booth will be set up where any visitor will be able to play several rhythm games:

- Play *Beat Saber* in a VR zone
- Play *Taiko no Tatsujin* and other arcade rhythm games using dedicated controllers
- Play a familiar circle-clicking game called *osu!*

### Quest book

![Quest book](/wiki/shared/news/2026-07-03-moscow-osu-event-2026/questbook.jpg)

The quest book holds lots of tasks to do, each giving you a stamp that you can later exchange for a prize. The more you complete, the sweeter your reward!

## Tickets

If you want to take part in the largest osu! event in Russia, you need to get yourself a ticket!

All information about tickets the can be found on [Moscow osu! Event's website](https://moscowosu.events/)!
