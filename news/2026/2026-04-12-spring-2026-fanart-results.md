Spring continues its run, and your creativity keeps pace — let's unveil the creations it inspired!

![](/wiki/shared/news/2026-04-12-spring-2026-fanart-results/banner.jpg)

With a total of **4,974** votes casted by **2,906** different users, we're excited to unveil the finalists of the Spring 2026 Fanart Contest!

*Note: You can click on any of the banners to view the full image.*

## Finalists

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/Triantafyllia.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/Triantafyllia.jpg)

[**Triantafyllia**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/17084594) made waves with a stunning painting of Mocha and Alisa against the sweetest of sunflowers and cherry blossoms. **1352** of you were impressed by such a flowery display, earning this entry the winning spot in this contest by just *two* votes!

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/nyrri.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/nyrri.png)

[**nyrri**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/32812061)'s inviting summer festival vibes with pippi, Mocha, Mani and Taikonator captured **1350** voters, drumming up enough hype to land the entry on the silver spot!

Being a new artist to enter this contest, congrats on making it this far on the first go!

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/KAITO%20thighs.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/KAITO%20thighs.png)

[**Dreamxiety**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/13103233), a familiar name in the osu! art scene, got Chirou and pippi to show **1209** picnic enthusiasts the wonders of roll cakes, pies, apples and tea on a relaxing spring day, bringing enough warm tones to gently and deservedly place this entry on the podium!

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/Porukana.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/Porukana.png)

[**Porukana**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/12992775) drew pippi and Alisa in a spring breeze that could be felt right through the screen, soaring into the hearts of **1064** people!

Making a return from last year's summer contest podium, this one too goes into the default profile page banners along with the top four this year.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/girls%20kissing.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/girls%20kissing.png)

A romantic entry brought to you by [**girls kissing**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/10060797) in collaboration with [**Creameries**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/15851364) managed to enthral **1060** of you with an exceptional composition of pearlescent atmosphere and popular headcanon that we cannot confirm the veracity of quite yet, quickly bubbling up to become a winner.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/RedcXca.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/RedcXca.png)

[**RedcXca**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/14056601)'s Mocha decided to spend a moment in a low-stress, nature-filled environment together with some unlikely guests. The greenery attracted **1032** nature lovers to nod in approval, comfortably shooting the entry up to sixth place.

Unfortunately, it has by rule 3 been vetoed from being featured in the main menu.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/tehfire.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/tehfire.jpg)

[**tehfire**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/7082924)'s dazzling scene of a touching moment between pippi and Mocha gave way for **1032** voters to instantly give their hearts for the entry that would surely end up near the top (and it did!).

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/Arushii.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/Arushii.jpg)

[**Arushii**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/15664628), like many other osu! artists, collectively agreed that a giant tablet pen is pippi's canon weapon of choice. Presented with another majestic view into pippi's adventures, **885** people recognised the travails before them and were left to wonder where it would lead them next.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/Kourihase.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/Kourihase.png)

[**Kourihase**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/13298982) brought everyone together and showed us a wonderful photo collection of memories past, mesmerising **864** reminiscent onlookers who didn't hesitate to praise every little detail.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/h3p0.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/h3p0.png)

[**h3p0**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/15875092) took on the spring picnic motif in style, incorporating vibrant greens, a familiar cat and a striking statue just beyond the stone gate, which **862** voters may or may not know the origins of. It's a mystery.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/Ferineon.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/Ferineon.jpg)

[**Ferineon**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/23930728) simply drew an exquisite dress for Mocha, and in the starlight her beauty resonated with **831** viewers who made spared no haste in including the art piece in their "favourites" collection.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/Sey10.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/Sey10.png)

[**Sey10**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/5843837) conveyed a relatable occurrence to osu! gamers: a sluggish school morning having played 15 too many beatmaps last night. Poor Mocha understands that time waits for no one, and so did **805** voters who wished pippi good luck on the day's studies and prayed it wasn't an exam day.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/nikio.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/nikio.png)

[**nikio**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/28895360) depicted the fantastical lives of a flying Chirou and Don passing by Mocha and an angelic pippi in a mild-contrast artwork that reached **712** star-struck voters.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/year-bear.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/year-bear.png)

[**year-bear**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/12178754)'s interpretation of osu!'s mascots as spring rabbits in wonderland. Complete with floating teapots and cutlery along with cute designs for every little trinket, there was no wonder in how **688** pairs of eyes gravitated towards the distinct art style.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/MPDisplay.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/MPDisplay.png)

[**MPDisplay**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/15424137) depicted a train ride that looks rather bleak, yet unimaginably beautiful. Both pippi and the flora are taking on the day with style and leaving **659** imaginative voters in awe.

## peppy's picks

It wouldn't be a fanart contest without [peppy](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/2) blessing us with some more visual delights for us to enjoy on the main menu!

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/Yonkoli.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/Yonkoli.png)

The spring theme came to [**Yonkoli**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/11415706) very naturally. The idea couldn't be simpler: pippi in a forest with flowing water and colourful flowers. Then we got a wild mix of colours, shadow patterns, big and small details, all coming together... naturally.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/-%20Y%20u%20m%20i%20J%20i-.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/-%20Y%20u%20m%20i%20J%20i-.jpg)

[**- Y u m i J i-**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/7656048)'s proud florist pippi stands out in a endearing painting-like art style, making for a piece that just couldn't be missed.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/Seml.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/Seml.png)

Glasses? Check. Mocha keychain? Check. Flower hairpin? Check. pippi is ready for the school day, and so were we to welcome [**Seml**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/25660611)'s fashionable drawing of her for display on the main menu.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/Sm0llBanana.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/Sm0llBanana.jpeg)

[**Sm0llBanana**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/21295136) envisioned pippi, Taikonator 3000 and Chirou having a relaxing time at a quaint pine forest creek, infusing a unique touch of lighting polish for an utmost soothing environment.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/wxyz.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/wxyz.jpeg)

[**wxyz**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/14954732)'s pippi and Mocha are walking downtown for the day, perhaps for an afternoon picnic?

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/Nellydoor.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/Nellydoor.png)

[**Nellydoor**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/13079223) made a unique collage of spring-themed artworks. We got fruits, several pippi compositions, various flower arrangements both in and around the frames. There are too many things endearing about this one that we never see in these contests, making it a special kind of winner.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/mimile1606.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/mimile1606.png)

[**mimile1606**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/37135521) looked at the [mascot wiki page](/wiki/Mascots) and didn't hold back, putting their cheerful style into *all* osu! mascots (plus some special guests). Everyone is in this art piece and they all fit into the picture in small unique ways.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/banners/THICC%20Jeff.jpg)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/282/winners/THICC%20Jeff.png)

[**THICC Jeff**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/17925852)'s pippi lied down in the grass and suddenly became one with nature, with many a manner of animal friends taking interest.
