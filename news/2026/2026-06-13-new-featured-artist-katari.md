The electronic scene's self-proclaimed "sparkly music maker" is now part of our Featured Artists!

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/549/header.jpg)

**Katari** is a Brazilian-American artist who began experimenting with music production nearly a decade ago. One side of **Katari** is inspired by doujin rhythm game artists like [*Camellia*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/31), [*seatrus*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/185) and [*EmoCosine*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/246), while the other side of **Katari** draws influence from a childhood of listening to Western electronic producers like *Virtual Riot*, [*Au5*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/312) and others from the [*Monstercat*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/255) and *NCS* rosters.

**Katari** has mastered their own form of genre fusion. This includes not only the intermingling of rhythm game and western electronic music, but also involves the fusion of orchestral, jazz, EDM, hardcore and many more genres in whatever ways **Katari** deems fit.

There are a total of **8** new tracks on [**Katari**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/549). Grab a `.osz`, mess around in the editor, then maybe one day you'll make a map as cool as any of these:
