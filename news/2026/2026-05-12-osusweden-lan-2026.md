The osu!Sweden community proudly presents the second iteration of its LAN events!

![](/wiki/shared/news/2026-05-12-osusweden-lan-2026/banner.jpg)

The osu!Sweden LAN is a 2-day in-person event taking place at Fryshuset Klubben, a venue situated in central Stockholm, from **July 3rd to July 5th**. During these two days, a variety of activities as well as a 1v1 tournament will be taking place! Come join us for all that sweet circle-clicking fun!

## The tournament

No LAN is complete without a 1v1 osu! tournament, and osu!Sweden LAN is no exception! A 1v1 RO16 single-elimination tournament will be hosted at the event, with players duking it out on stage in front of everyone at the venue!

Prior to the event, you'll be able to sign up and play qualifiers online. The 16 highest-seeded players advance to the bracket stage, which will be played live on stage. Make sure to bring the peripherals you're most comfortable with — the competition will no doubt be fiercer than anything Sweden has seen before!

![Players playing on stage at osu!Sweden LAN 2025](/wiki/shared/news/2026-05-12-osusweden-lan-2026/stage.jpg)

If you are unable to attend and don't want to miss the show, you can follow the bracket stage matches live on our [Twitch channel](https://www.twitch.tv/sweden_cup)!

## Additional activities

In addition to the main tournament, other smaller activities will take place during the event. Not only will the LAN feature the classic pp-leaderboard, tracking everyones performance throughout the event — you'll also be able to compete against your fellow circle-clickers in our osu!-themed quiz! On top of that, we have a couple more activities planned, but they're secret for now!

Rumour has it that you'll be able to compete in these activities to win some merch featuring our mascot, Månviva! (The stylish girl you see at the top of this article together with her cat, Minion.)
