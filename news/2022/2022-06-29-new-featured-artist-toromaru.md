We've rounded up **12** songs from modern jazz savant **Toromaru** for our Featured Artist library!

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/279/header.jpg)

If you ever thought rhythm game music wasn't complicated enough, **Toromaru** is here prove otherwise. Drawing inspiration from classic rock and jazz piano, **Toromaru**'s intricate sound has traversed the music world into games like *SOUND VOLTEX*, *pop'n music*, *CHUNITHM*, and (of course) **osu!**.

A dozen of these masterpieces can be found on [**Toromaru**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/279)!

If you need some mapping inspiration, [this beatmap of Formless Canvas](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/1727694) demonstrates exactly how **Toromaru**'s elaborate sound translates to circles and sliders.
