Falling leaves and crisp cool air, make way for autumn's golden flare...

![](/wiki/shared/news/2024-10-09-autumn-2024-fanart-results/banner.jpg)

A staggering **2840** of you have distributed a grand total of **42737** votes across **181** entries! We've tallied up the votes and are now ready to unveil what **you've** chosen as the finalists that will grace osu!'s main menu for the next few months.

### Finalists

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/221/banners/KAITO%20thighs.png)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/221/winners/KAITO%20thighs.png)

Securing a 6th victory (and an 8th podium finish) for this contest, [**KAITO thighs**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/13103233)'s (better known as Dreamxiety) art piece of pippi taking a rest with a picnic impressed **1550** people, perfected by a cute [pippi plush](https://osu.ppy.sh/home/news/2022-12-10-makeship-x-osu-plushies) peeking her head from a basket.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/221/banners/leeian.png)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/221/winners/leeian.png)

**1419** art connoisseurs felt the autumn breeze in [**leeian**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/12595640)'s painting of pippi engrossed in observation.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/221/banners/Type.png)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/221/winners/Type.jpg)

[**Type**](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/7520534)'s elegantly stellar Alisa commanded the attention of **1309** onlookers over the cloudy cityscape.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/221/banners/Skieih.png)](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/221/winners/Skieih.png)

---

As always, none of this would've been possible without all of you passionate individuals who took precious time to participate in this contest, alongside everyone who subsequently participated in the voting process. You're the lifeblood of this contest.

If any of these mesmerising pieces have piqued your interest, [you can download a zip file containing all entries of the Autumn 2024 Fanart Contest here](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/221/Autumn2024FanartSubmissions.zip)!

If you're an [osu!supporter](/wiki/osu!supporter), take this opportunity to drag your favourite artwork right into the game window, making it the main menu background even if it wasn't featured in the seasonal batch. Else, you'll instead get to enjoy the top 3 entries as [**limited-time default profile banners**](https://osu.ppy.sh/home/news/2024-07-15-aerial-antics-art-results#new-profile-customisations), which will be available to use until the next fanart contest's results.

You'll also find the full list of entries in the [contest listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/contests/221) if that's more your thing.
