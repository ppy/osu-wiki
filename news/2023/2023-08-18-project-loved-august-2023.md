Summer might be coming to an end, but Project Loved returns to keep the good times going!

![](/wiki/shared/news/banners/project-loved-2.jpg)

For everyone who's not yet familiar with who we are and what we do, Project Loved hosts community voting polls each month to decide which beatmaps get added to the [Loved category](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets?s=loved). All of the maps are chosen by the [Loved captains](https://loved.sh/team) of their respective game modes. Each beatmap banner in this news post will redirect you to the respective poll, or you can view them all directly on the [Project Loved forum](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/120). The voting process lasts for 10 days, so don't forget to vote! More info can be found on the [wiki page](/wiki/Community/Project_Loved) and [website](https://loved.sh).

As is tradition by now, we would like to thank everyone for your participation in the July votes. If you would like to view the results from the previous round, you may do so by clicking the following links:

- [Results for osu!](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/posts/9218511)
- [Results for osu!taiko](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/posts/9218510)
- [Results for osu!catch](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/posts/9218509)
- [Results for osu!mania](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/posts/9218508)

If you haven't joined them yet, there are dedicated hubs for Project Loved if you want to hang out with other people and discuss various aspects of Project Loved. You can click the banners below to join the Discord servers for your game mode of choice!
