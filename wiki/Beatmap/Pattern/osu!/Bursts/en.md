# Bursts

**Bursts** are a higly used pattern in [beatmaps](/wiki/Beatmap). They consist of 3 or more [hit circles](/wiki/Gameplay/Hit_object/Hit_circle) that are [stacked](/wiki/Beatmapping/Mapping_techniques/Stack) on eachother.
Bursts mostly consist of 1/4 or faster rythms.
