# 6K Autumn Showdown Tournament

![6K AST banner](img/banner.jpg)

The **6K Autumn Showdown Tournament** (***6K AST***) was a 1v1, double-elimination, global osu!mania 6-key tournament hosted by ::{ flag=NL }:: ::Albionthegreat::{ user-id=9853595 }, ::{ flag=US }:: ::tastydumpl1ng::{ user-id=18496063 } and ::{ flag=SG }:: ::TheFunk::{ user-id=13981991 }. It was the first instalment in the 6K AST series.

## Ruleset

### Round Overview

#### Qualifiers

1. The Qualifiers Stage will happen in the first week of the tournament play. All of the registered players who were not screened out during screening are eligible to participate in the Qualifiers.
2. Each player can play the Qualifiers in sequence up to 2 times (players may opt to skip the 2nd run).
3. The Qualifiers pool format will have 8 stages, and they are as followed:
   - Stage 1: Accuracy/Tech
   - Stage 2: Speed/Delay
   - Stage 3: Early Mixed Hybrid
   - Stage 4: LN Release
   - Stage 5: Mixed Rice
   - Stage 6: Chordstream
   - Stage 7: LN Density
   - Stage 8: Complex Mixed Hybrid
4. If you played the Qualifiers twice, the higher score for each map will be taken.
5. Each player's seed is determined by how well they perform on each map. The 8 maps will be averaged out and any players that performed well for all of the maps will get a higher seeding. Only the Top 64 seeded players will proceed on to the bracket stage, while the lower seeded players will be eliminated.

#### Bracket Stage

1. All matches will be played on Score V2.
2. All maps are played with Free Mod turned ON. Visual mods such as Mirror, Hidden, Fade In, Flashlight, and any combination of these mods are allowed. No Fail is allowed too.
3. Bracket Matches will arrange all the players into a standard Double Elimination Bracket, where 1st seed will face against 64th seed, 2nd seed will face against 63rd seed, and so on.
4. The Mappool format for the bracket stages will be as follows:

| Round | Maps | Format |
| --: | :-: | :-- |
| Round of 64 | 10 maps | Best of 7 |
| Round of 32 | 12 maps | Best of 9 |
| Round of 16 | 12 maps | Best of 9 |
| Quarterfinals | 14 maps | Best of 11 |
| Semifinals | 14 maps | Best of 11 |
| Finals | 16 maps | Best of 13 |
| Grand Finals | 16 maps | Best of 13 |

### Match Procedure

1. Room settings are `Game mode: "osu!mania"`, `Score mode: "ScoreV2"`. Room name will be `6KAST: Qualifier Q#` for Qualifiers, and `6KAST: ({Player 1}) vs ({Player 2})` for every bracket stage matches.
2. Players are required to be present before their match time. If one player isn't present during the match time, a 10 minute grace period will start by the referee.
3. If the player fails to join the lobby during the 10 minute grace period, the match will be considered as a win by default to the other player. If both players fail to show up on time, the higher seeded player will win by default.
4. In advance, players will send their warmups (if they have one) to the referee of their match. Warmups must be 6K and have less than 4 minutes of drain time.
5. After warmup has concluded, both players will run the `!roll` command in the lobby chat. The player with the higher roll will be able to choose whether they want to protect first, ban second and pick first, or protect second, ban first and pick second.
6. During the match, both the players will alternate in picks depending on who picked first, and play the picked map together. The player with the higher score will obtain a point.
7. Players will be given 2 minutes to protect and 2 minutes to ban a map from their choosing. Later on, players will have 2 minutes to pick a map and 2 minutes to get ready for the map.
8. If a player fails to Protect/Ban/Pick during the given time, the referee will choose a map at random to be picked in behalf of the player by using the `!roll` command.

### Disconnections

1. If a player gets disconnected from a map during the first 30 seconds of the gameplay, they can ask for the map to be aborted and replayed. This is only available once per match for each player, any subsequent disconnects will award the other player a point.
2. If a player gets disconnected from a map after the first 30 seconds of the gameplay, the point will be awarded to the other player.
3. If a player is unable to return to the lobby within 10 minutes, the match will be considered a win for the other player.
4. If both players are disconnected, the player that comes back will be saved from losing. If the other player comes back before their grace period ends, the match will continue. This will only be available once. If subsequent cases happen, the player that joins back will be considered the winner of the match.
5. If both players are unable to reconnect to the lobby in time, the current standing will determine the winner of the match. In the case of a tie, the higher seeded player will be awarded the win.
6. The hosts reserve the right to be more or less strict at their discretion.
