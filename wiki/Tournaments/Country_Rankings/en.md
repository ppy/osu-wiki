# Country Rankings

Countries that participated in the various osu!standard, osu!taiko, osu!catch and osu!mania World Cup tournaments are shown here as well as their rank if they made it in the top 3. There are 31 official tournaments as of now.

*Note: Some teams are not listed here because they were not representing any countries. Teams with two or more countries may not show here. There are numbers in parenthesis on "Total number of teams" that indicate the teams shown (not the actual).*

## Marks

| Mark | Explanation |
| :-: | --- |
| ![][true] | Participated in tourament. |
| ![][false] | Did not participate in tournament. |
| (+N) | Participated with N countries as a single team. |
| (xN) | Country had N teams. |
