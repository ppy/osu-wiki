# Sparxe's Two Key Event

This is the index page for all tournaments in the Sparxe's Two Key Event (*STKE*) series. Tournaments in the STKE series feature 2-key maps for the [osu!mania](/wiki/Game_mode/osu!mania) game mode.

Closely related to Sparxe's Two Key Event and organised in part by the same team is the [Jaye's One Key Event](/wiki/Tournaments/JOKE) tournament series, featuring 1-key maps instead.
