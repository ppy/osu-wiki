The **Muted** mod reduces music and [hitsound](/wiki/Beatmapping/Hitsound) volume as the player's [combo](/wiki/Gameplay/Combo_(score_multiplier)) increases, eventually muting both entirely. As the volume decreases, a metronome beat fades in (if this is enabled in [Customisation](#customisation)). [Breaking combo](/wiki/Gameplay/Judgement/Combobreak) resets the volume to its normal level.

## Customisation

![Muted customisation options in the game client](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier_(lazer)/img/customise/MU.png)

- **Start muted** (default: disabled): Reverse the effect of the mod. Volume will start muted, and increase with combo. If this setting is enabled, the **Final volume at combo** setting's minimum value is 1 instead of 0.
- **Enable metronome** (default: enabled): Add a metronome beat that gets louder as the music gets quieter.
- **Final volume at combo** (0–500, default: 100): The combo at which the final volume will be reached. If set to 0 and **Start muted** is disabled, the volume will always be muted.
- **Mute hit sounds** (default: enabled): Hitsound volume will be affected in addition to music volume.

Changing any of these settings will **not** cause your scores to be unranked.
