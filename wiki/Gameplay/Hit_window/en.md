# Hit window

![](img/hit_window.png "Exemplary scheme of a hit window visualising the possible outcomes of a hit")

Each [hit object](/wiki/Gameplay/Hit_object) has a time frame called **hit window** in which players are required to hit them. The higher the accuracy of the hit, the more score will be rewarded. Failing to hit the hit object at all will incur a miss.

The length of a hit window is affected by the [overall difficulty](/wiki/Beatmap/Overall_difficulty) as well as rate-changing mods like [Double Time](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Double_Time) and [Half Time](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Half_Time).

osu!catch has no concept of timing, so any hit objects will be judged as either hit or missed.
