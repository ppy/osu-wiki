# X

osu! has several accounts on **X** (formerly Twitter), each serving a specific purpose.
Most are not very active, but they help keep you informed outside the osu! ecosystem.

| ![osu!status avatar](img/osustatus.jpg) | [@osustatus](https://x.com/osustatus) | Notifications about website issues and service outages. See also [Bancho](/wiki/Bancho_\(server\)). |
