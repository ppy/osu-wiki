Bancho
==========

Bancho has a variety of meanings. Depending on the context, it may refer to:
-   [Bancho](/wiki/Glossary#Bancho): The server coded by peppy to handle all [osu!](/wiki/Glossary) connections.
-   [BanchoBot](/wiki/BanchoBot): The resident bot of osu! [Chat Console](/wiki/Chat_Console) that helps both players and staff.
